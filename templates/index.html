01 — ABOUT COLLEGE

A college focused on
learning and student success.

Established in 1973, Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College is one of the well-known colleges in Visakhapatnam. Affiliated with Andhra University and accredited with NAAC ‘A’ Grade, the college provides quality education with experienced faculty, modern facilities, and good placement support.

Modern Campus

Smart classrooms, labs, and library facilities for students.
